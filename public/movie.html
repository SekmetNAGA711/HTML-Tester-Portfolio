The best Movies to watch according to Nana Abena

Best Movies I've watched: 2023 - 2026

Dune

Dune is one of the best modern sci-fi films because of its incredible world-building, stunning visuals, and powerful soundtrack. The movie makes you feel fully immersed in the universe while delivering a deep story about power, destiny, and survival.

Wicked

Wicked is a fantastic musical that tells the untold story of the witches of Oz. The performances are outstanding, and the music is catchy and emotional. It’s a must-watch for fans of musicals and fantasy.

One of Them Days

One of Them Days is a powerful and emotional film that explores themes of love, loss, and resilience. The storytelling is compelling, and the performances are heartfelt, making it a memorable cinematic experience.

Avatar: Fire and Ash

Avatar: Fire and Ash is a visually stunning sequel to the original Avatar film. It expands on the rich world of Pandora while delivering an engaging story about environmentalism, family, and courage. The special effects are breathtaking, making it a must-see for fans of sci-fi and adventure.
